HPC Quest

      The HPCQuest was my first big programming project. At the time I had 2 months of programming experience and did not know what to expect. It was an extremely difficult but rewarding journey. It was supposed to be a group project but the other members in my team were not as motivated or as adventurous as me, so I ended up doing all of the work myself. I did however delegate some extra work to them though so that they stuck with it.

      About

      This project was a competition organized by the A* Star research institute in Singapore to engage Junior College students and introduce them to research. The A*star institute was a government founded research institute that was the largest one in Asia. The project itself was split into 3 parts: Implementation, Parallelization and Visualization (bonus). We were given a dataset containing 10 cell parameters and had to normalize and group them according to the clusters they formed.
